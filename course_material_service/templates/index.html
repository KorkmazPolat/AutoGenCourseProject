Quick Tips

Guide the AI toward a convincing script by framing the essentials clearly.

  • Lead with a short, benefit-driven course title.
  • Keep learning outcomes measurable and action oriented.
  • Match the tone to how you would speak to your learners.
  • Leave voice/model blank to use the default studio narrator.
  • Need to iterate? Submit again—the newest video replaces the previous one.
